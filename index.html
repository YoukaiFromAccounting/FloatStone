The Float Stone is a unique held item that reduces the weight of the Pokémon holding it by half. This remarkable stone appears to defy gravity itself, floating gently in the air and emanating a soft, ethereal glow.

When held by a Pokémon, the Float Stone's mysterious properties significantly reduce the effectiveness of weight-based moves used against the holder, while also making the Pokémon feel lighter and more agile in battle.

Effect
Halves the holder's weight
Type
Held Item
Rarity
Uncommon
First Appeared
Generation V

Strategic Uses:

  • Reduces damage from weight-based moves like Heavy Slam and Heat Crash
  • Increases the power of moves like Grass Knot when used by the holder
  • Essential for certain Pokémon strategies involving weight manipulation
  • Particularly useful for naturally heavy Pokémon that want to avoid weight-based attacks
